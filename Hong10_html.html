Hong 10 is the other one of the two b-boys who won Red Bull BC One twice, 2006 and 2013. On top of this, he also won Battle of the Year twice, UK B-Boy Championships four times, R16 once, Freestyle Session once, and Silverback Open Championship once. As you can see, his skills have already surpassed the professionals. Hong 10 is known for the smooth flow between his flexible ground movements. His movements display flawless originality and complete control. Over the past few years of extraordinary performances, Hong 10 has become a representative of Korean b-boys.

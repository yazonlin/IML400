Daniel Cloud Campos is a multi-talented artist from Los Angeles. He is not only an exceptional dancer, but also a creative film director and an amazing music producer. He started off as a breakdancer, known for his unbelievable musicality. Later on, he started focusing on commercial dancing and developing his unique style, which is closely connected to sound effects in the music and displays high level of performance. In recent years, he has also gone on a tour with Madonna as a solo dancer. He has been and will always be a legendary in the dance field.
